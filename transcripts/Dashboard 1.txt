"Role: You are a senior data visualization engineer and BI dashboard expert specializing in executive-level reporting. You build production-grade, interactive dashboards that transform raw transactional data into boardroom-ready intelligence.
Context: I am presenting this dashboard to the CEO and CFO of Bajaj. It must be clean, authoritative, and immediately interpretable — zero guesswork for a time-poor executive. The data spans 4 years (2018–2021) of US retail transactions with 9,994 orders across 3 product categories (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology), 3 customer segments (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office), 4 regions (East, West, Central, South), and 17 sub-categories. Key financial metrics include Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Discount.
Task: Build a fully interactive HTML + JavaScript dashboard (single self-contained file) with the following specifications:

SECTION 1 — KPI Summary Bar (top of page)
Display 5 metric cards in a horizontal row:

Total Revenue: $2.84M
Total Profit: $286K
Profit Margin: 10.1%
Total Orders: 9,994
Unique Customers: 793
Each card shows the metric name, value in large bold font, and a subtle trend arrow or color indicator.


SECTION 2 — Global Filter Panel (sticky or top-positioned)
Interactive slicers that filter ALL charts simultaneously:

Year selector (2018, 2019, 2020, 2021, or All)
Region multi-select (East, West, Central, South)
Segment multi-select (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
Category multi-select (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology)
Ship Mode filter (Same Day, First Class, Second Class, Standard Class)


SECTION 3 — 10+ Charts (described below)
Build these charts using Chart.js or D3.js. Each chart must respond to the global filters:

Monthly Sales & Profit Trend (Line Chart) — Dual-axis line chart showing monthly Sales (left axis) and Profit (right axis) over time. Helps CEO spot seasonal patterns and peak periods.
Sales by Category (Donut Chart) — Furniture vs Office Supplies vs Technology. Show % share and absolute value. CFO can instantly see revenue concentration.
Profit Margin by Sub-Category (Horizontal Bar Chart) — Ranked from highest to lowest margin. Color-code bars: green if margin > 15%, amber if 5–15%, red if negative. This is a critical profitability view for the CFO.
Regional Performance (Bar + Line Combo Chart) — Grouped bars for Sales per region, overlaid with a Profit Margin % line. East vs West vs Central vs South.
Customer Segment Revenue Split (Stacked Bar Chart by Year) — Shows Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office revenue stacked per year (2018–2021). Reveals growth trajectory of each segment.
Sales vs Profit Scatter Plot (Bubble Chart) — Each bubble = one sub-category. X-axis = Total Sales, Y-axis = Total Profit, Bubble size = Total Quantity sold. Color by Category. Instantly shows which products drive volume vs value.
Discount Impact Analysis (Line Chart) — X-axis = Discount % bands (0%, 10%, 20%, 30%, 40%, 50%), Y-axis = Average Profit. Shows the inflection point where discounts destroy margin — critical CFO insight.
Top 10 States by Revenue (Horizontal Bar Chart) — Clean ranked list. Color by Region. Add a small profit margin % label at the end of each bar.
Shipping Mode Distribution & Cost Impact (Pie + KPI) — Pie chart of orders by ship mode. Below it, show average days to ship and average profit per mode as a small table. Helps CEO evaluate logistics strategy.
Year-over-Year Revenue Growth (Waterfall or Grouped Bar) — Show annual Sales and Profit side-by-side for 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021. Add % YoY growth labels above each bar.
(Bonus) Monthly Orders Heatmap (Calendar Heatmap) — Rows = months, Columns = days or weeks. Cell color intensity = number of orders. Reveals peak ordering periods. Green color ramp.


SECTION 4 — Automated Insights Panel (right sidebar or bottom row)
Generate 5 dynamic text insights that update when filters change. Examples:

"Technology drives the highest profit margin at 17.4%, but Furniture has the lowest at 2.5%."
"Discounts above 20% result in negative average profit — currently applied to 18% of orders."
"The West region generates the most revenue ($725K) but Central has the weakest margins."
Style these as card-based callout boxes with an icon prefix (warning, info, success).:
